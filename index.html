The real problem is not the paperwork. It’s the hidden process behind the paperwork.

Most vendor onboarding delays happen because the packet looks complete when it isn’t. Requirements are buried, dependencies are implicit, and teams only discover missing items after review starts. ReadyPath reconstructs the process itself so teams can act before the rework begins.

Without ReadyPath
  • Teams guess what the packet is actually asking for
  • Missing items show up late after internal review already started
  • Suppliers bounce between unclear requests and repeated follow-up
  • No shared definition of what “submission ready” means
With ReadyPath
  • A clean requirements map from messy packet inputs
  • A missing-items list before submission gets blocked
  • Ambiguity and risk flags surfaced early
